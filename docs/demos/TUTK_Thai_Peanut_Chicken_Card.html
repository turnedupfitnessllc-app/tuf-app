INGREDIENTS

6 oz skinless chicken breast

2 tbsp peanut butter

2 tbsp Bragg's soy alternative

1 tbsp lemon juice

1 tbsp rice vinegar

¼ tsp cayenne pepper

1 cup mixed veggies of choice

DIRECTIONS
🥜
01

Microwave peanut butter slightly. Whisk with Bragg's, lemon juice, rice vinegar & cayenne until thick.

🍗
02

Shred or slice cooked chicken into thin pieces. Prep your choice of mixed veggies.

🥗
03

Toss chicken and veggies in sauce. Top with salt, green onion, or sesame seeds. Serve immediately.

💡 PRO TIP

Add sriracha or chili oil for extra heat. Serve over cauliflower rice for a lower-carb option.

🔬 40+ SCIENCE

Peanut butter's monounsaturated fats support joint lubrication — critical after 40. The lean protein from chicken helps maintain muscle mass as metabolism slows with age.
